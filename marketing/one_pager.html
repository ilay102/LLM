How it works

1 · Intelligent routing

Classifier picks the right model per request: cheap (Haiku, gpt-4o-mini) for classification & extraction, balanced (Sonnet) for most tasks, frontier (Opus) only when you need it.

2 · Semantic caching

Identical and paraphrased prompts hit a local cache. ~25% of typical SaaS traffic comes back in 3 ms instead of 2 s — at zero cost.

3 · Provable quality

Pairwise LLM-as-a-judge eval shows you exactly how the cheaper routes stack up against your current setup. We refuse to ship anything that hurts your output.
